About Cadi Atelia

Excellence in Architecture Since 1999

Our Story

Founded in 1999, Cadi Atelia has established itself as a premier architecture and construction firm, known for creating extraordinary spaces that blend timeless elegance with contemporary innovation. Our journey began with a simple vision: to transform architectural dreams into magnificent realities.

Over 25 years, we've completed 150+ projects, from luxury residential developments to iconic commercial complexes. Each project represents our unwavering commitment to excellence, attention to detail, and collaborative partnership with our clients.

Today, with a team of 50+ talented architects, designers, and engineers, we continue to push the boundaries of what's possible in architecture and construction.
